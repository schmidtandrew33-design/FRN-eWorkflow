Notification Routing

Manage delivery channels, recipient defaults, and timing behavior for workflow messages generated by the FRN approval system.

Delivery Channels

Stage Start Notification
Notify the assigned reviewer when a document enters a new stage and is ready for work to begin.
Ready for Approval Notification
Alert the next approver when a document has finished review and is ready for approval action.
Document Approved Notification
Send confirmation when a document is approved and advanced to the next stage of the workflow.
Document Rejected Notification
Notify document owners and current stage reviewers when a document is rejected and returned for correction.
