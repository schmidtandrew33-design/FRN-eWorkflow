Dr. Sarah Chen Dec 3, 2025 at 10:45 AM
In Section 4.2, the exposure assessment methodology needs clarification. Can you provide more detail on the sampling protocol used for environmental monitoring?
Michael Torres Dec 3, 2025 at 2:15 PM
Good question. The sampling protocol follows EPA Method 8270D. I'll add a reference to the methodology appendix in the next revision.
Dr. Sarah Chen Dec 3, 2025 at 3:20 PM
The risk characterization in Table 7 shows uncertainty factors, but the confidence intervals seem narrow given the data variability. Can you explain the statistical approach?
Dr. Robert Martinez Dec 3, 2025 at 4:05 PM
I agree with Dr. Chen's concern. Additionally, the bioaccumulation factors in Section 5.3 need verification against the updated 2025 EPA guidance document.
Michael Torres Dec 3, 2025 at 5:30 PM
We used Monte Carlo simulation with 10,000 iterations. I'll expand the statistical methods section and cross-reference with the 2025 guidance. Will have this updated by tomorrow morning.
Dr. Sarah Chen Dec 4, 2025 at 9:00 AM
Section 6.1 references outdated toxicity data from 2022. Please update with the most recent IRIS assessment published in August 2025.
Dr. Sarah Chen Dec 4, 2025 at 11:30 AM
Question about the sensitivity analysis: Were ecosystem services impacts considered in the economic valuation model?
Michael Torres Dec 4, 2025 at 1:45 PM
Yes, ecosystem services were included using the EPA Eco-SAM framework. It's detailed in Appendix C, but I can add a summary to the main text for clarity.
